WHO ARE WE

About Us

Welcome to WeCare, where our mission is to empower every child, regardless of their background, to reach their full potential. Through compassionate initiatives, we provide underprivileged children with vital support, including access to education, healthcare, and safe environments for growth.
Our diverse programs, such as educational scholarships and mentorship schemes, address the specific needs of each child, ensuring they have the tools to pursue their dreams. We believe that investing in children is investing in our collective future, and with the support of donors, volunteers, and partners, we're shaping a world where every child can thrive and make a positive impact. Join us in creating a brighter tomorrow filled with hope and opportunity for all children. Welcome to WeCare.

Our History

In the early 2000s, a group of individuals founded WeCare with a mission to address social issues like poverty and education inequality. Through grassroots initiatives and strategic partnerships, WeCare provided essential services to communities worldwide. Notably, their "Empowerment Through Education" program, launched in 2008, aimed to break the cycle of poverty by offering education and resources to underprivileged children. By the end of the 2000s, WeCare had become a respected international charity, known for its commitment to positive change. Through empathy, integrity, and solidarity, WeCare inspired hope and inclusivity in communities globally.
